## Termination Clause

### Termination for Convenience

The Company may terminate this contract for convenience with 30 days written notice to the Vendor.

### Termination for Cause

The Company may terminate this contract immediately for cause in the following circumstances:

1. **Material Breach**: Vendor's material breach of contract terms
2. **Non-Performance**: Failure to meet agreed deliverables or milestones
3. **Insolvency**: Vendor's insolvency, bankruptcy, or appointment of receiver
4. **Legal Violations**: Violation of applicable laws or ethical standards
5. **Misrepresentation**: Material misrepresentation in proposal or during execution

### Vendor's Right to Terminate

Vendor may terminate with 60 days written notice if Company fails to make payments as per agreed terms after written notice and 30-day cure period.

### Consequences of Termination

Upon termination:
- Vendor shall immediately cease all work
- Vendor shall deliver all completed and in-progress work to Company
- Company shall pay for work completed and accepted up to termination date
- Both parties shall return confidential materials
- Survival clauses (confidentiality, IP, indemnification) remain in effect
