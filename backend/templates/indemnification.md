## Indemnification

### Vendor's Indemnification

The Vendor shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Company, its affiliates, officers, directors, employees, and agents from and against any and all claims, damages, losses, liabilities, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from or relating to:

1. **Intellectual Property Infringement**: Any claim that the deliverables or services infringe upon third-party intellectual property rights
2. **Personal Injury or Property Damage**: Personal injury or property damage caused by Vendor's negligence or willful misconduct
3. **Breach of Confidentiality**: Unauthorized disclosure or use of Company's confidential information
4. **Violation of Laws**: Vendor's violation of applicable laws, regulations, or industry standards
5. **Employee Claims**: Claims by Vendor's employees or subcontractors
6. **Data Breach**: Security breaches or data loss caused by Vendor's systems or processes

### Company's Indemnification

The Company shall indemnify the Vendor against claims arising from:
- Company's breach of contract
- Misuse of deliverables by Company beyond the scope of the license granted

### Indemnification Process

1. The indemnified party must promptly notify the indemnifying party of any claim
2. The indemnifying party shall have the right to control the defense and settlement
3. The indemnified party shall cooperate in the defense
4. No settlement shall be made without the indemnified party's consent if it imposes obligations on them

### Limitations

Indemnification obligations do not apply to claims arising from the indemnified party's own negligence or willful misconduct.
