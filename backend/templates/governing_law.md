## Governing Law

### Applicable Law

This contract shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India, without regard to its conflict of law provisions.

### Jurisdiction

The courts of Kolkata, West Bengal, India shall have exclusive jurisdiction over any disputes arising out of or relating to this contract.

### Language

All communications, notices, and documentation related to this contract shall be in the English language. In case of any translation, the English version shall prevail.

### Compliance with Laws

Both parties shall comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards in the performance of their obligations under this contract, including but not limited to:
- Companies Act, 2013
- Income Tax Act, 1961
- Goods and Services Tax (GST) Act
- Information Technology Act, 2000
- Labor laws and employment regulations
- Environmental protection laws
- Industry-specific regulations

### Severability

If any provision of this contract is found to be invalid or unenforceable by a court of competent jurisdiction, such provision shall be severed from the contract, and the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

### Waiver

No waiver of any provision of this contract shall be deemed or shall constitute a waiver of any other provision, nor shall any waiver constitute a continuing waiver.
