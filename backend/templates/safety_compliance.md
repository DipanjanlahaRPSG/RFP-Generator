## Safety & Compliance

All vendors must comply with:

1. **Labor Laws**: All applicable labor laws and regulations of India
2. **Safety Protocols**: Company safety protocols when on premises
3. **Data Protection**: Data protection and privacy regulations including IT Act, 2000
4. **Environmental Guidelines**: Environmental guidelines as applicable to the scope of work
5. **Industry Standards**: Relevant industry standards and best practices
6. **Workplace Safety**: Occupational health and safety requirements

### Compliance Requirements

- Vendors must maintain all necessary licenses and certifications
- Regular compliance audits may be conducted
- Non-compliance may result in immediate contract termination
- Vendors must report any safety incidents within 24 hours
