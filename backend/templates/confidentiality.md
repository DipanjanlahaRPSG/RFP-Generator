## Confidentiality

### Confidential Information

- All information disclosed by either party in connection with this engagement shall be considered confidential
- Confidential information includes but is not limited to: technical data, business strategies, customer information, financial data, and proprietary processes

### Obligations

1. **Non-Disclosure**: Vendor must maintain strict confidentiality of all Company information
2. **NDA Requirement**: A separate Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) must be executed before sharing detailed requirements
3. **Limited Use**: Information can only be used for purposes directly related to this engagement
4. **Protection**: Vendor must implement reasonable security measures to protect confidential information
5. **Return of Materials**: All confidential materials must be returned or destroyed upon contract termination

### Duration

- Confidentiality obligations survive contract termination for a period of 5 years
- Certain obligations may be perpetual as specified in the NDA

### Exceptions

Confidential information does not include information that:
- Is publicly available through no fault of the receiving party
- Was rightfully in possession prior to disclosure
- Is independently developed without use of confidential information
- Is required to be disclosed by law or court order
