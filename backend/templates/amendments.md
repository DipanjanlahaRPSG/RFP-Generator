## Amendment Procedures

### Written Amendments Only

All amendments, modifications, or changes to this contract must be:
- Made in writing
- Signed by authorized representatives of both parties
- Attached to and made part of the original contract

### Oral Modifications Invalid

No oral modifications, amendments, or changes to this contract shall be valid or binding on either party.

### Change Request Process

1. **Initiation**: Either party may initiate a change request by submitting a written proposal
2. **Review**: The other party shall review the proposal within 10 business days
3. **Negotiation**: Both parties shall negotiate the terms, scope, timeline, and cost implications
4. **Documentation**: Agreed changes shall be documented in a formal amendment
5. **Execution**: Amendment must be signed by authorized signatories before implementation

### Change Control

For changes to scope, deliverables, or timeline:
- Impact assessment must be conducted
- Cost and schedule implications must be documented
- Both parties must agree to revised terms before proceeding

### Authority

Only the following individuals have authority to approve amendments:
- For the Company: [Authorized Signatory as per contract]
- For the Vendor: [Authorized Signatory as per contract]

### Record Keeping

All amendments shall be:
- Numbered sequentially (Amendment No. 1, 2, 3, etc.)
- Dated and referenced to the original contract
- Maintained as part of the contract file
- Provided to all relevant stakeholders

### Integration

This contract, together with all executed amendments, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, or agreements.
