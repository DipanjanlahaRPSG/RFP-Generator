## Liability Limitations

### Maximum Liability

Except as provided below, the total aggregate liability of either party under this contract shall not exceed the total contract value paid or payable under this agreement.

### Exclusion of Consequential Damages

Neither party shall be liable for any indirect, incidental, consequential, special, or punitive damages, including but not limited to:
- Loss of profits or revenue
- Loss of business opportunities
- Loss of data (except as covered under data breach provisions)
- Cost of substitute services
- Damage to reputation

### Exceptions to Limitations

The liability limitations do not apply to:

1. **Fraud or Gross Negligence**: Claims arising from fraud, willful misconduct, or gross negligence
2. **Intellectual Property Infringement**: IP infringement claims and related indemnification
3. **Confidentiality Breach**: Breach of confidentiality obligations
4. **Data Protection Violations**: Violations of data protection laws and regulations
5. **Personal Injury**: Death or personal injury caused by negligence
6. **Indemnification Obligations**: Indemnification obligations under this contract

### Insurance Requirements

Vendor must maintain adequate insurance coverage as specified in the Insurance Requirements section to support these liability provisions.

### Survival

These liability limitations shall survive termination or expiration of the contract.
