## Dispute Resolution

### Negotiation

In the event of any dispute arising out of or relating to this contract, the parties agree to first attempt resolution through good faith negotiation for a period of 30 days.

### Mediation

If negotiation fails to resolve the dispute, the parties agree to submit the matter to mediation by a mutually agreed mediator. The mediation shall be conducted in Kolkata, India.

### Arbitration

If mediation fails to resolve the dispute within 60 days, the matter shall be referred to arbitration:

- **Governing Law**: Indian Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996
- **Number of Arbitrators**: Single arbitrator mutually appointed by both parties
- **Seat of Arbitration**: Kolkata, India
- **Language**: English
- **Award**: The arbitrator's decision shall be final and binding on both parties

### Costs

Each party shall bear its own costs for negotiation and mediation. Arbitration costs shall be borne as determined by the arbitrator.

### Continued Performance

During dispute resolution proceedings, both parties shall continue to perform their obligations under the contract to the extent not directly affected by the dispute.
